Escalation Policy

Mandatory safety guidelines for peer volunteers.

Immediate Escalation Required

Peer volunteers are not permitted to handle crisis, self-harm, or suicidal ideation cases independently. These situations must be escalated without delay.

Escalate When You Observe:

  • Mentions of self-harm or suicide
  • Severe emotional distress or panic
  • Threats of harm to self or others
  • Repeated expressions of hopelessness

Escalation Procedure

  1. Stop peer-level engagement immediately
  2. Notify assigned counsellor or administrator
  3. Document the concern clearly and objectively
  4. Do not attempt continued support beyond reassurance

Peer volunteers act as support facilitators only. This platform does not replace professional mental health services.
