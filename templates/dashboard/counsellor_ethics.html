Professional Integrity

Ethics & Escalation

Confidentiality

All student data and assessment scores must remain strictly confidential unless a high-risk trigger is identified.

Boundaries

Counsellors must maintain professional boundaries. Use the guidance system for one-way support only.

Immediate Escalation Protocol

If a student exhibits self-harm tendencies or "High Severity" scores:

  • Contact the University Emergency Response Team immediately.
  • Flag the assessment report for Admin review.
  • Do not attempt to handle life-threatening crises via text-only guidance.
  • Direct the student to the 24/7 Helpline: 9152987821
